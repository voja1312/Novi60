About Dr. David Steiner

22+ Years of Experience in Chiropractic Care

Dr. David Steiner is a highly respected chiropractor based in Fort Myers, Florida, dedicated to helping patients achieve optimal health through natural, non-invasive approaches to pain relief and wellness.

With over two decades of clinical experience, Dr. Steiner combines traditional chiropractic techniques with cutting-edge therapeutic modalities to create personalized treatment plans that address the root cause of his patients' conditions, not just their symptoms.

Known for his compassionate and results-driven approach, Dr. Steiner takes time to listen to each patient's concerns and educate them about their condition, empowering them to take an active role in their healing process.

Professional Credentials

  • Doctor of Chiropractic, Palmer College of Chiropractic
  • Board Certified by the National Board of Chiropractic Examiners
  • Advanced Certification in Sports Injury Management
  • Member of the American Chiropractic Association
  • Florida Chiropractic Association, Active Member
